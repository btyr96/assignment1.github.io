Highlights


Going OM

Why go home when you could be 'Going OM'? Going OM is inspired by the spirit of the Himalaya mountains. The serenity and harmonious energies were what inspired them to set up this sanctuary in Singapore. A car/bar inspired by the Himalays serving Indian fusion food, Vietnamese coffee, live music, yoga and meditation classes all under one roof. Going OM could just be your second h'OM'e.

Druken Ballon

Drink, Drank, Drunk...Drunken Ballon. Art, bags, clothes, accessories, games, novels...I am starting to think they have it all. Get ready your wallet because I can guarantee you will not leave the store empty handed!

Juice Clinic

Chug them booze and get loose at Juice Clinic. Juice Clinic is not only for the alcoholic. They serve juice (duh), coffee, lamb chops, rainbow cheesecake and many more. Admire the pop art-inspired graffiti while you enjoy your Sunday brunch.
